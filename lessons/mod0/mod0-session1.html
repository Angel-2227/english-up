Grammar Spotlight — Verb To Be ⭐
⏱ 8 min
📌 Find Verb To Be in the song:
There he was, this young boy…
A stranger to my eyes. (He was a stranger.)
I felt all flushed… I was embarrassed.
PronounPresent (now)Past (before)
Iamwas
He / She / Itiswas
You / We / Theyarewere
✏️ Quick Practice — fill in with am / is / are / was / were
1. He _______ a stranger to my eyes. (past)
2. The music _______ beautiful tonight. (present)
3. They _______ in the crowd last night. (past)
4. I _______ so embarrassed right now! (present)
5. We _______ strangers before this class. (past)
✅ Answers: 1. was   2. is   3. were   4. am   5. were

7
Discussion — Let's Talk! 💬
⏱ 8 min
Answer in English — there are no wrong answers!
  • 1
    How does "Killing Me Softly" make you feel? Choose a word: sad / calm / happy / nostalgic / romantic / moved.
    💡 Try: "The song makes me feel ___" or "I feel ___ when I hear it."
  • 2
    Have you ever felt embarrassed in public? What happened? Where were you?
    💡 Try: "Yes! I was embarrassed when ___. I was at ___." or "No, I'm never embarrassed!"
  • 3
    The title says "Killing me softly." But she doesn't die! What do you think it really means?
    💡 There's no wrong answer — be creative! "I think it means ___ because ___."
  • 4
    Do you know a song in Spanish or another language that tells a story? What is the story about?
    💡 Try: "I know a song called ___. It is about ___. It makes me feel ___."
